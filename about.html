About Fit Buddy

How It Works

Fit Buddy is paired up with fours gyms in the UBC area to provide students with a comparison that would best fit their needs. The data collected displays prices for various memberships, gym opening hours, available trainers, and location that we thought would best suit students' needs *NOTE: Due to copyright reasons, we could not use the real names of these gyms so they are fictious but based on real gyms in UBC
